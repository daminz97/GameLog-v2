GameLog

GameLog is an online auto game information logging and analyses system. It is cross-platform and offers easy-to-understand analytics results for the gaming experience. People who are interested in playing games, no matter their gaming levels, or simply want to track their gaming time and expenses, can conveniently use this website. Gaming platforms are disconnected from each other, users cannot connect with people from another platform even they play the same game. GameLog provides a unique experience for gamers to connect, play and analyze.
